README - H2O BLOB
Patrick Lathan, Elena Sparacio, Nathan Young <Money> 

GAME OVERVIEW:

The goal of the game is save your GrandBlob! He is freezing to death because of the climate and Blobothermia. You need to find all 3 magical plants to save your GrandBlob. The player must run around the screen

The player find all 3 magical seeds without killing himself or his GrandBlob. If they complete this task, they will beat the game. Otherwise, it's not looking good for the GrandBlob... The UI shows the player their ice powers and the number of magical seeds they have collected. 

CONTROLS:

Player Movement: Arrow Keys or WASD
Jump: Spacebar
Move Camera: Mouse or trackpad movement
Ice Powers: Click mouse or trackpad 


FEATURES:

Explore a vast, freezing world to find seeds.
Play as a super cute and snazzy blob.
Use ice powers in unique ways to find seeds.
Ability to murder your GrandBlob if you so choose. 

KNOWN BUGS:

The camera zooms out with lots of rotation.
The camera allows you to see below the playable area.
The arrow keys reverse.
The game sometimes randomly starts far away from the player.
The ice and jump powers in combination make the player invincible
Many wanted features are missing.
